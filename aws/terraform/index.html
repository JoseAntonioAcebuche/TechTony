PTK Sanduguan

PTK Sanduguan is a Pekiti Tirsia Kali martial arts group located in Cavite. It is headed by Tuhon Jake, with dedicated members including Guro Tony and Guro Enzo. The group focuses on training, discipline, and preserving the authentic Filipino martial art of Kali.

Our Discipline

Pekiti Tirsia Kali is a close-quarter combat system from the Philippines, emphasizing edged and impact weapons, empty-hand combat, and tactical strategy.

About PTK Sanduguan

PTK Sanduguan was founded to preserve and promote the Pekiti Tirsia Kali system in Cavite. With guidance from Tuhon Jake, the group instills discipline, respect, and mastery of Kali among its members.

Training Focus

  • Knife and sword drills
  • Single and double stick techniques
  • Empty-hand applications
  • Combat strategy and tactics

Instructors

  • Tuhon Jake — Head Instructor
  • Guro Tony — Instructor
  • Guro Enzo — Instructor

Together, they uphold the tradition and authenticity of Pekiti Tirsia Kali.
